The problem

There are more sustainability projects than most portfolios can fund at once.

Commercial real estate owners may be considering solar, batteries, HVAC upgrades, heat pumps, insulation, EV charging, building automation, water management and biodiversity measures.

The challenge is not finding possible projects. It is deciding which projects should happen first, at which assets, and why.

How projects are prioritised

Sustainability impact and commercial value are assessed together.

01

Financial impact

Indicative investment, operating savings, payback logic and financing considerations.

02

Carbon and energy impact

Potential emissions reduction, energy performance improvement and contribution to sustainability targets.

03

Asset value

Marketability, resilience, tenant appeal, future relevance and obsolescence risk.

04

Strategic relevance

Alignment with portfolio plans, regulation, reporting obligations and owner priorities.

05

Technical readiness

Building condition, grid capacity, available space, timing and infrastructure constraints.

06

Evidence quality

Data completeness, confidence levels, assumptions and the specialist input still required.
